Customer Guide

Everything you need to know as a customer visiting the Spare Parts Portal — from finding the part you need to receiving a quotation.

👋 Welcome

This is an industrial spare parts portal. Unlike a regular online shop, you do not see prices or place instant orders here. Instead, you:

  1. Browse or search for the parts you need.
  2. Add them to your Enquiry Cart.
  3. Submit your details — and we send you a price quotation within 24 hours.
ℹ️ No account needed. You do not need to create an account or log in to submit an enquiry. Just browse and send your request.

🔄 How It Works

1

Browse or Search

Visit the Shop page or the Homepage to find parts by category, part code, or machine name.

2

Add to Enquiry Cart

Click "Add to Enquiry" on any part you want. You can add multiple parts from different categories before submitting.

3

Submit Your Details

When ready, go to your Enquiry Cart and click "Submit Enquiry." You'll fill in your name, email, phone number, and any special notes.

4

Receive Quotation

Our team reviews your enquiry and sends a pricing and availability quote to your email (or WhatsApp) within 24 hours.

🔍 Browsing the Parts Catalogue

There are several ways to find the part you're looking for:

Option A — By Category

1

Click a Category on the Homepage

The homepage shows a rotating carousel of part categories (like Bearings, Gears, Motors, etc.). Click any category to see all parts in that group.

2

Use the Left Sidebar on the Shop Page

When you're on the shop page, the left column shows all available categories. Click any to filter.

Option B — Use the Shop Page

Go to Shop from the top navigation. You'll see all available parts. Use the left sidebar to sort by Newest, Name A–Z, or Popular.

💡 Tip: If you know the part code (e.g. SP-BEAR-001) or machine model (e.g. Sulzer G6300), type it in the search bar for the fastest results.

🔎 Searching for a Part

1

Find the Search Bar

Look for the search bar in the website header or on the Shop page. It's the box that says "Search parts…"

2

Type What You Know

You can search by:
• Part name — e.g. "deep groove ball bearing"
• Part code — e.g. "SP-BEAR-001"
• Machine name — e.g. "Sulzer" or "G6300"

3

Browse the Results

Matching parts will appear. Each card shows the part name, a badge with the part code, and the machine it fits.

💡 Can't find it? Don't worry — just use the Contact page and describe the part (or attach a photo). Our team can identify and source almost any spare part.

📄 Understanding a Part's Detail Page

Clicking on any part card takes you to a full detail page. Here's what you'll find:

ℹ️ About Prices: Prices are not shown on the product page. This is by design — prices depend on quantity, current availability, and your location. You'll receive a formal price quote after submitting your enquiry.

🛒 Using the Enquiry Cart

The Enquiry Cart works like a shopping cart, but instead of buying, you're creating a list of parts you want pricing for.

1

Add Parts

On any part page or in the shop listing, click "Add to Enquiry". The cart icon in the header will show how many items are in your cart.

2

Open Your Cart

Click the cart icon in the top navigation bar. You'll see all parts you've added, with their part codes and quantities.

3

Adjust Quantities

In the cart, you can change the quantity for each part (e.g. if you need 5 bearings, type 5). The quantity updates automatically when you change the number.

4

Remove a Part

Click the red trash icon (🗑️) next to any part you don't want anymore.

5

Continue Browsing

You can click "Continue Shopping" to go back to the shop and add more parts before submitting.

📤 Submitting Your Enquiry

1

Click "Submit Enquiry"

In your Enquiry Cart, click the orange "Submit Enquiry" button. A form popup will appear.

2

Fill In Your Details

The form asks for:
• Full Name — your name or contact person name
• Email Address — where we send the quotation
• Phone / WhatsApp Number — for quick follow-up
• Company Name — optional
• Message / Special Requirements — add any notes like "urgent, need within 3 days" or "need OEM part only"

3

WhatsApp Confirmation

There's a checkbox: "Send me a confirmation on WhatsApp." Keep this checked if you want a WhatsApp message confirming we received your enquiry.

4

Submit

Click "Submit Enquiry". You'll see a green confirmation message with your Enquiry Number (e.g. ENQ-0001). Keep this number for your records.

✅ After submitting: You'll receive a confirmation email. Our team receives an instant notification and will respond with a quote within 24 hours.

⏳ What Happens After You Submit

Once your enquiry is submitted, our team receives an alert and works on your case. Here is the journey your enquiry goes through:

1

Enquiry Received New

Your enquiry has been received and is in our queue. You'll get a confirmation email shortly.

2

Under Review Acknowledged

Our team has reviewed your part list and is checking stock availability and pricing.

3

Quotation Sent Quoted

A formal quotation has been emailed to you. It includes pricing, availability, and lead time for each part.

4

Closed Closed

The enquiry is complete — either fulfilled or concluded.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account?
No. You can browse the catalogue and submit an enquiry without creating an account. Just provide your contact details in the enquiry form.
Why don't I see prices?
Prices for industrial spare parts vary depending on the quantity you need, current stock availability, your location, and delivery requirements. We send you an accurate quotation after reviewing your specific requirement.
How long does it take to receive a quotation?
We aim to respond within 24 hours on working days (Monday to Saturday, 9 AM – 6 PM). For urgent requirements, WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us.
What does "Available on Request" mean?
Some parts are not kept in stock but can be sourced. "Available on Request" means we don't have it right now, but we can procure it. Contact us for lead time.
Can I enquire about a part I can't find on the website?
Absolutely! Use the Contact page or WhatsApp us with the part code, machine name, or a photo. Our team will identify and source the part for you.
Can I request multiple parts in one enquiry?
Yes. Add all the parts you need to your Enquiry Cart and submit them all at once. This is the recommended way — you get a single consolidated quotation for all parts.
What information should I include in my enquiry?
The more details, the better. Include: part name, part code (if you know it), machine name and model, quantity needed, and any urgency notes. A photo is very helpful too.
I submitted an enquiry but haven't heard back. What should I do?
First, check your spam/junk folder. If there's nothing after 24 hours (on a working day), contact us directly on WhatsApp or phone using the number on the Contact page.
