Step 01

Logging into the Admin Panel

Access your store's backend to manage everything from a single, powerful dashboard.

1

Open the Admin Login Page

Open your web browser and navigate to the admin login URL. This is a separate area from the customer-facing storefront.

http://yoursite.com/E-com/admin/login
2

Enter Your Credentials

Type your admin email address and password in the login form. These credentials are provided by the system administrator or were set during initial setup.

3

Click "Login" to Access the Dashboard

After entering valid credentials, click the Login button. You'll be redirected to the admin dashboard where you can see an overview of your store's performance.

Step 02

Understanding the Dashboard

Your command center — see all key metrics at a glance including orders, revenue, and alerts.

Stats Cards (Top Row)

Four key metric cards displayed at the top showing:

  • Total Orders received
  • Total Customers registered
  • Total Revenue earned
  • Total Products in catalog

Monthly Sales Chart

An interactive bar + line chart showing monthly revenue and order count. Hover over any bar to see the exact figures. This helps track sales trends over time.

Quick Stats Panel

A sidebar panel showing urgent items:

  • Pending Orders awaiting processing
  • Orders currently being processed
  • Low Stock items that need restocking
  • Pending Reviews to approve

Best Sellers & Recent Orders

See your top-selling products with images, prices, and sold count. Below that, a full table of recent orders with order number, customer, total, payment status, order status, and date. Click any row to see details.

Step 04

Managing Products

Add new products, edit pricing, manage images, and organize your entire catalog.

1

Go to Products Page

Click "Products" in the sidebar under the Catalog section. You'll see a filterable table of all products with image, name, SKU, category, price, stock level, status, and featured badge.

Sidebar → Catalog → Products
2

Use Filters to Find Products

At the top, you can search by name or SKU, filter by category, filter by status (Active/Inactive), and sort by newest, price, or name. Click "Filter" to apply, or "Reset" to clear.

3

Add a New Product

Click the blue "+ Add Product" button in the top-right corner. Fill in the product form: name, description, price, sale price, SKU, stock quantity, category, images, and featured status. Click Save.

4

Edit or Delete Products

Each product row has action buttons: click the ✏️ pencil icon to edit, or the 🗑️ trash icon to delete (with confirmation prompt). When editing, you can change any product field and re-upload images.

Step 05

Managing Orders

Track, filter, and process all customer orders from placement to delivery.

1

Navigate to the Orders Page

Click "Orders" in the sidebar under Sales. You'll see a table with: Order #, Customer name & email, Item count, Total amount, Payment status, Payment method, Order status, and Date.

Sidebar → Sales → Orders
2

Filter Orders

Use the filter bar to search by order number or customer name, filter by status (Pending, Confirmed, Packed, Shipped, Delivered, Cancelled), filter by payment status (Pending, Paid, Failed, Refunded), and set a date range.

3

View Order Details

Click the 👁️ eye icon on any order to open its detail page. Here you can see the full order breakdown — items ordered, shipping address, payment details, and timeline. You can update the order status (e.g., move from "Pending" → "Confirmed" → "Shipped" → "Delivered").

Step 06

All Admin Features

Deep-dive into every module available in your admin panel.

Inventory Management

Quickly adjust stock levels for any product without opening the full edit form.

  • Search products by name or SKU
  • See color-coded stock badges (green/yellow/red)
  • Use +, −, or = to add, subtract, or set stock
  • Dedicated "Low Stock Alerts" page

Coupon Management

Create discount campaigns to boost sales and customer engagement.

  • Percentage (%) or Fixed Amount coupons
  • Set minimum order amount requirement
  • Usage limits (total uses cap)
  • Expiry date with auto-expiration
  • Performance tracking dashboard

Payments Tracking

Monitor all payment transactions across multiple gateways.

  • View by method: Razorpay, Stripe, PayPal, COD
  • Distribution cards showing totals per method
  • Filter by status, method, and date range
  • Full Transaction Log with IDs

Reports & Analytics

Data-driven insights to grow your business.

  • Sales Report: Revenue, orders, avg. value with charts
  • Product Report: Best sellers, performance data
  • Customer Report: Registration trends, activity
  • Time period filters: 7, 30, 90, 365 days

SEO Management

Optimize your store for search engines directly from the admin.

  • Set meta title & description per product
  • Set meta title & description per category
  • Add meta keywords for search ranking
  • Configure Open Graph (OG) images

Site Settings

Configure global store settings grouped by category.

  • Store name, currency, contact info
  • Settings auto-grouped (General, Shipping, etc.)
  • Long values show as text areas
  • One-click "Save Settings" for all changes
Step 07

Scrolling, Tables & Navigation Tips

How to use scrollable tables, horizontal overflow, and quick navigation in the admin panel.

Horizontally Scrollable Tables

All admin tables (Products, Orders, Payments, Customers, etc.) are wrapped in a responsive scrollable container. On smaller screens or when columns are wide, you can scroll left/right by dragging or swiping horizontally. The table headers remain visible as you scroll down.

Pagination Controls

Large data sets are split across multiple pages. When there are more items than one page can show, pagination buttons appear at the bottom of the table. Click page numbers or Next/Previous arrows to navigate between pages. The current page is highlighted.

Mobile Sidebar Toggle

On mobile or tablet devices, the sidebar collapses automatically. Tap the ☰ hamburger icon (three horizontal lines) at the top-left to open/close the sidebar navigation. The sidebar slides in as an overlay and can be dismissed by tapping the ✕ close button.

Clickable Table Rows

In the Dashboard's "Recent Orders" table, you can click any entire row to navigate directly to that order's detail page. The cursor changes to a pointer to indicate clickable rows. This works as a quick shortcut instead of finding the "View" button.

Modal Pop-ups (Categories & Coupons)

Categories and Coupons use modal pop-ups for creating and editing entries. Click "Add Category" or "Add Coupon" and a form slides up over the page. Fill it out and submit without leaving the page. The page content underneath is dimmed and unscrollable until you close the modal.

Sidebar Scroll & Active States

The sidebar itself is scrollable if there are many items. The currently active page is highlighted in blue in the sidebar so you always know where you are. Submenus (like Reports) expand/collapse with an arrow indicator.

Step 08

Pro Tips & Best Practices

Expert advice to get the most out of your admin panel.

Check the Dashboard Daily

Start each day on the Dashboard. Review pending orders, low-stock alerts, and new customer registrations so nothing falls through the cracks.

Always Confirm Deletions

All delete actions show a confirmation prompt. Read it carefully — deleted products, categories, and coupons cannot be recovered once confirmed.

Use Featured Products

Mark your best products as "Featured" using the star toggle. Featured products appear prominently on the homepage and attract more clicks.

Organize Categories

Create parent categories first (e.g., "Electronics"), then add sub-categories (e.g., "Laptops", "Phones"). A clean hierarchy helps customers browse.

Monitor Low Stock

Visit Inventory → Low Stock Alerts regularly. Set appropriate thresholds so you're alerted before popular items sell out completely.

Optimize SEO Early

Set meta titles and descriptions for products and categories right from the start. Good SEO brings organic traffic and reduces your marketing spend.
